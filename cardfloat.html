Gadhwal

Garhwal, located in the northern state of Uttarakhand in India, is a region rich in natural beauty, cultural heritage, and historical significance. Known for its picturesque landscapes, towering mountains, and spiritual centers, Garhwal is a destination that captivates visitors with its serene charm and vibrant traditions. Geography and Climate Garhwal is characterized by its rugged terrain, featuring the majestic peaks of the Himalayas, deep valleys, and flowing rivers like the Ganges and Yamuna. The region experiences a varied climate, with cold winters and mild summers in the higher altitudes, while the lower areas enjoy a more temperate climate. This diverse climate supports a range of flora and fauna, making it a paradise for nature enthusiasts.
